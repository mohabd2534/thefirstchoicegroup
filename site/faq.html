SELLING TO US
How is your offer calculated?
We price against recent comparable sales — Zillow, local MLS, and county records — then adjust for size, unit count, and condition. You see the preliminary range instantly; a specialist confirms the firm number within 24 hours.
Are there really no fees or commissions?
Really. No commissions, no fees, and we pay all closing costs. The offer you accept is the amount you walk away with — we'll put it in writing.
How fast can you actually close?
Our average is 26 days, and we've closed in as little as 10. Because we pay cash, there's no appraisal, no lender, and no financing contingency to slow things down. Need longer? You pick the date.
What does "as-is" really mean?
No repairs, no cleaning, no cleanout. Fire damage, failed septic, decades of belongings — we've bought it all. Take what you want and leave the rest.
Is the preliminary estimate binding?
No — it's a data-driven starting range, free and yours to keep. The firm written offer follows a quick call and one walkthrough, still with zero obligation.
Am I better off listing with an agent?
Sometimes, yes — and we'll tell you when. For a move-in-ready house with time to spare, a listing can net more. When speed, condition, or privacy matter, cash usually wins after commissions and carrying costs. Ask us for the side-by-side math.
